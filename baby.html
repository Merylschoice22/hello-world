A Goodnight Poem

Time for me to go to bed,
Hope I don't wake up instead.
As I lay, I'm missing you,
In the big arms of my boo.
Tomorrow I can hold you tight,
Don't squeeze too hard, I just might.
So into dreamland now I fall,
Ready to escape it all.
The best thing would be pleasant dreams,
And not wake up with piercing screams.
So wish me luck now as I go,
Until we meet again, tomorrow.
